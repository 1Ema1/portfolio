Comic book

I used a fortress Old Castle which is a part of Old Town Varazdin.The architecture of the castle gave me an idea since every tower is designed a little different. If they were people, they would have different personalities. To make the comic fun, I decided that those personalities would be of the seven dwarfs (Grumpy, Happy, Sleepy, Bashful, Sneezy, Dopey and Doc).

Photo recycle

This assignment was to create image anarchy on a topic of our choosing. I used Adobe Photoshop for raster graphics, and Freepik - for finding the best available graphics to use.
